## Photography Guidelines

- **Lighting**: Natural daylight preferred, avoid harsh midday shadows
- **Composition**: Rule of thirds, include full roof lines when possible
- **Safety**: Crew should wear proper PPE in all action shots
- **Branding**: Include branded elements (trucks, shirts, signs) where natural
- **Quality**: Minimum 72 DPI for web, shoot at highest camera resolution
- **Releases**: Obtain photo releases for all identifiable people and properties
- **Consistency**: Maintain consistent color temperature across all photos
- **File Names**: Use descriptive names: `shingle-roof-replacement-christiansburg-01.jpg`
