**SERVICE AGREEMENT**

*Website Design, Development & Ongoing SEO Services*

**Date:** February 12, 2026

**Service Provider:** Social Theory Media (“Provider”)

**Client:** Modern Day Roofing (“Client” / “MDR”)

This Agreement outlines the scope of services, deliverables, payment terms, and ownership rights for the website redesign and ongoing digital marketing services to be provided by Social Theory Media for Modern Day Roofing.

# **1\. Scope of Work**

## **1.1 Website Design & Development**

Provider will design and develop a custom website for Client using the following technology stack:

* Frontend Framework: Astro (open-source, free)

* Content Management System: Sanity CMS

* Hosting Platform: Vercel

* Version Control: GitHub

The website build will include:

* Full website redesign with mobile-first, responsive design (desktop, tablet, mobile)

* Homepage designed as a conversion-optimized customer journey funnel

* Two location-specific hub pages (Roanoke area and New River Valley area) with unique, non-duplicate content

* Approximately 15 location-specific SEO pages

* Service pages with sub-pages (roof replacement, roof repair, maintenance, gutters) including breakdowns by roofing type (shingle, metal, timber seal, etc.)

* Financing page with Hearth widget integration

* Education hub / blog section

* About Us page with bios and company information

* Contact page

* Storm damage page optimized for ad conversion

* 15 pre-loaded keyword optimized evergreen blog posts targeting common pain points

## **1.2 Content Management System (CMS) Setup**

Provider will configure Sanity Studio with the following capabilities:

* Modular page builder with reusable content blocks (Hero, CTA, Reviews, Forms, Services Grid, FAQ, Text \+ Image, Testimonials, Stats/Awards Banner, Financing, etc.)

* Structure Builder for organized Studio navigation

* Live Preview / Visual Editing via Sanity Presentation Tool

* AI Content Assistant (Sanity AI Assist plugin) for content generation and editing assistance

* Blog management system with full publishing workflow

Client will have full autonomy to create new pages, add/reorder/remove content blocks, edit text and images, and publish content without developer involvement.

## **1.3 SEO & Technical Optimization**

* Full 301 redirect mapping from existing WordPress URLs to new site

* XML sitemap and robots.txt configuration

* Schema markup / structured data

* Breadcrumb navigation

* Lazy loading for images and media

* Page speed optimization (targeting sub-second load times)

* Google Analytics and Meta Pixel integration

* Hotjar integration for user behavior tracking

* ADA compliance best practices

## **1.4 Forms & Lead Capture**

* Custom form system to replace Gravity Forms functionality (if needed)

* Per-page form source tagging (so Client knows which page/location generated each lead)

* Spam protection (reCAPTCHA, honeypot fields, server-side filtering)

* Form submissions routed to Client’s CRM and/or email

* GCLID/FCLID capture from URL parameters stored with form submissions

* Desktop and mobile-optimized form behavior (right-side form on desktop, CTA button to form on mobile)

## **1.5 Additional Features**

* Google Reviews integration displaying live review data from Google My Business

* Exit-intent and scroll-triggered popup functionality for lead capture / promotional offers

* Sticky mobile CTA bar (Call Now \+ Get a Quote)

* Staging environment for previewing changes before publishing

* Automated testing via Playwright to check for broken links, 404 errors, and functionality after updates

* GitHub-based backup protocol with code saved on every update

# **2\. Ongoing Monthly Services**

Beginning upon launch of the completed website, Provider will deliver the following on a recurring monthly basis:

## **2.1 SEO Blog Content**

* Two (2) SEO-optimized blog posts per week (approximately 8 per month)

* Content based on keyword research targeting customer pain points and search queries relevant to the roofing industry

* Evergreen content strategy designed to drive organic traffic and improve search rankings

* Posts published directly to the website via Sanity CMS

## **2.2 Website Maintenance & Support**

* Ongoing website maintenance including bug fixes, minor updates, and performance monitoring

* Monitoring for broken links, 404 errors, and site health

* Assistance with CMS questions or content management support

* Minor content or design adjustments as reasonably requested

*Note: Major new features, integrations, or significant redesign work beyond routine maintenance will be scoped and quoted separately.*

# **3\. Project Timeline**

Provider will deliver a first draft of the complete website within approximately one (1) week of receiving all necessary content, assets, and access credentials from Client.

Following the first draft delivery, Client and Provider will collaborate on revisions and refinements. Provider will make reasonable revisions based on Client feedback until both parties are satisfied with the final product.

The target for full website launch is within 3–4 weeks of project kickoff, subject to timely feedback and content delivery from Client.

# **4\. Payment Terms**

| Item | Amount | When Due |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Website Design & Development | **$3,000.00** | Due upon signing |
| Monthly Retainer (SEO Blog Content \+ Maintenance) | **$500.00/month** | Begins upon website launch |

* The upfront payment of $3,000.00 is due upon execution of this Agreement and covers the full website design and development described in Section 1\.

* The monthly retainer of $500.00 begins upon launch of the completed website and is billed monthly via Stripe invoice.

* The upfront payment will be made via check. Monthly retainer payments will be processed via Stripe invoice.

* Monthly retainer may be cancelled by either party with 30 days’ written notice.

# **5\. Platform Ownership & Code Control**

All platforms, accounts, and source code associated with this project will be owned by and billed directly to Client:

* The GitHub repository containing the website source code will be created under a Modern Day Roofing-owned account.

* The Sanity CMS project and organization will be set up under a Modern Day Roofing-owned account, with any subscription fees billed directly to Client.

* Vercel hosting will be configured under a Modern Day Roofing team account, with any hosting fees billed directly to Client.

* Client will have full access to the complete, production-ready source code at all times.

In the event this Agreement is terminated, Client retains full ownership of all code, content, accounts, and platforms. No assets are dependent on Provider’s accounts or infrastructure.

**Estimated third-party platform costs (billed directly to Client, not to Provider):**

* Sanity CMS: Free tier available; Growth plan at $15/user/month if AI Assist and scheduling features are desired

* Vercel: Free tier available for most use cases; Pro plan at $20/month if additional bandwidth is needed

* Domain: Client’s existing domain registration (moderndayroof.com) — no change required

# **6\. Client Responsibilities**

To ensure timely delivery, Client agrees to:

* Provide all necessary content, images, logos, brand assets, in a timely manner

* Provide access to existing WordPress site, Google My Business, CRM, and any third-party platforms requiring integration (Hearth, Pin Parrot, etc.)

* Provide timely feedback on drafts and deliverables (within 3–5 business days)

* Set up and maintain MDR-owned accounts for GitHub, Sanity, and Vercel with Provider’s guidance

# **7\. Revisions & Change Requests**

Provider will work collaboratively with Client throughout the build process and will make reasonable revisions to ensure the website meets Client’s expectations.

Significant changes to the project scope (e.g., additional pages beyond what is outlined, new integrations not discussed, or major design overhauls after approval) may be subject to additional fees, which will be communicated and agreed upon in writing before work begins.

# **8\. Confidentiality**

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary business information, customer data, login credentials, or trade secrets shared during the course of this engagement. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement.

# **9\. Limitation of Liability**

Provider will exercise reasonable care and professional skill in performing the services described herein. Provider’s total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client to Provider under this Agreement. Provider is not liable for any loss of revenue, data, or business opportunity arising from website downtime, third-party service outages, or circumstances beyond Provider’s reasonable control.

# **10\. Termination**

* Either party may terminate the monthly retainer with 30 days’ written notice.

* If Client terminates the Agreement prior to website launch, the upfront fee of $3,000.00 is non-refundable, as it compensates Provider for work performed.

* Upon termination, Provider will transfer all remaining access, credentials, and deliverables to Client within 7 business days.

* All code, content, and platform ownership remains with Client regardless of termination.
