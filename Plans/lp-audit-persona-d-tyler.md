- **GAF** → "(the largest U.S. roofing manufacturer)" on first use
- **Tear-off vs overlay** → "Tear-off = we remove your old roof completely (we always do this)"
- **Flashing** → "(metal seals around chimneys, vents, and walls)"
- **Pipe boots** → "(rubber seals around roof vents)"
- **Underlayment** → "(waterproof membrane under the shingles)"
- **Ice & water shield** → "(extra waterproofing in the valleys where two slopes meet)"
- **Ridge vent** → "(ventilation strip at the peak)"
