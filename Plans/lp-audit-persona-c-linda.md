## Top 5 issues

**1. CRITICAL: Form renders before headline on mobile.** `order-1 lg:order-2` on form card + `order-2 lg:order-1` on copy = mobile sees form FIRST. Skeptical 61yo just burned by pushy salesman sees form demand before reading a single trust signal. Immediate back-button.
- Fix: Swap — headline `order-1`, form `order-2` on ALL screens.

**2. Branded LP has zero second-opinion language.** Linda searched "2nd opinion Salem" and landed here. No mention of competitor quote review anywhere.
- Fix: Add benefit card: "Got another quote? Bring it. We'll tell you if the math adds up." + FAQ: "Can you review a quote from another company?"

**3. No way to attach the competing quote.** Linda has a PDF. Form asks name/phone/email/service. She has to wait for call + re-explain. Friction.
- Fix: Add note: "Text a photo of your quote to (540) 553-6007 before the call" (low-dev). Or file upload field when `source` contains `competitors`.

**4. Missing "$9k-$18k typical range" on competitors LP.** Linda was quoted $18,400. This specific data point is in roof-replacement FAQ but NOT on competitors LP where it's most useful.
- Fix: Surface price range prominently on competitors page.

**5. No Salem-specific signal despite ?city=Salem substitution.** Headline reads "Salem Neighbors Actually Recommend" but body has no Salem-texture.
- Fix: "We work in Salem every week" or "47 roofs completed in Salem and the Roanoke Valley".
