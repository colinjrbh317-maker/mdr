### 2.6 Notable Industry Examples

## 5. MDR Differentiation Strategy

### The GAF Master Elite Advantage

MDR's GAF Master Elite certification is the cornerstone differentiator. Here is why and how to leverage it:

**The Facts:**
- Only ~2% of all roofing contractors in North America qualify for GAF Master Elite
- Among the 5 competitors analyzed, only Big Lick Roofing and Peak Roofing also hold this certification
- The three largest competitors (Skywalker, Cenvar, Best Choice) hold Owens Corning Platinum Preferred -- a different manufacturer's top tier

### Design Direction Recommendations

Based on cross-competitor analysis:

1. **Color Scheme:** Dark primary background (navy or charcoal) with a bold, warm accent color (gold, amber, or orange -- distinct from Cenvar's green, Skywalker's cyan, Best Choice's red, Big Lick's blue, and Peak's green)
2. **Typography:** Modern sans-serif, clean and highly readable. Large headline text for hero sections.
3. **Photography:** Original, professional project photography. Drone aerials of completed work. Team photos in branded gear. Avoid stock images (customers recognize stock).
4. **Layout:** Card-based service grids. Full-width sections with alternating background colors. Generous white space. Mobile-first grid system.
5. **Tone:** Confident but approachable. Lead with expertise ("Top 2%"), follow with warmth ("We live where you live"). Avoid corporate jargon. Speak to homeowners in plain language.
