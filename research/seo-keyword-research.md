## 1. Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive keyword research strategy for Modern Day Roofing as it expands from its Christiansburg base into the Roanoke market. The strategy is built on three pillars:

1. **Local dominance** -- Owning the search results for every service area city and county name combined with core roofing terms.
2. **High-intent capture** -- Targeting transactional keywords that indicate a homeowner is ready to hire (emergency repairs, replacement quotes, insurance claims).
3. **Content authority** -- Building topical authority through question-based content that answers what Southwest Virginia homeowners actually search for.

**Key market data points:**
- 46% of all Google searches have local intent
- "Near me" roofing searches convert at over 40%
- Over 70% of roofing leads come from mobile devices

- Long-tail keywords account for over 90% of all Google searches

- Emergency roofing keywords convert at a much higher rate than normal keywords
