## 4. Seasonal Keyword Strategy

Virginia has distinct seasonal search patterns for roofing. Align content publishing and ad spend accordingly.

### Spring (March–May) — PEAK DEMAND BEGINS

**Keywords spike:**
- "roof inspection after winter"
- "spring roof maintenance checklist virginia"
- "roof damage from winter virginia"
- "roof replacement spring virginia"
- "storm damage roof repair [city]"

**Action:** Publish maintenance/inspection content in February. Ramp up PPC budgets in March. Push storm damage landing pages live before spring storms.

### Summer (June–August) — PEAK STORM SEASON

**Keywords spike:**
- "emergency roof repair [city]"
- "storm damage roof repair [city]"
- "hail damage roof repair [city]"
- "roof insurance claim virginia"
- "roof leak repair [city]"

**Action:** Monitor NOAA weather alerts. Be ready to boost storm damage PPC within hours of major weather events. Social media posts showing rapid response build trust.

### Fall (September–November) — SECONDARY PEAK

**Keywords spike:**
- "roof replacement before winter"
- "best time to replace roof virginia"
- "metal roof installation [city]"
- "gutter installation [city]"
- "roof inspection [city]"

**Action:** Fall is ideal installation weather (moderate temps, low humidity). Push replacement-focused content. Offer fall inspection specials.

### Winter (December–February) — SLOW SEASON / PLANNING

**Keywords spike:**
- "ice dam roof damage virginia"
- "roof leak winter virginia"
- "roof replacement financing"
- "how much does a new roof cost"
- "signs you need a new roof"

**Action:** Focus on informational/planning content. Offer off-season pricing incentives. Build content library for spring push.

## 5. Competitor Keyword Intelligence

### Top Competitors & Their Keyword Focus

**Cenvar Roofing** (largest regional competitor)
- Title format: "[City]'s BEST Roofing Contractor | Roof Replacement | Cenvar Roofing"
- Keywords targeted: "best roofing contractor," "roof replacement," city names
- Weakness: Generic claims, no specialty services (no slate, no Timber Seal)
- Has dedicated pages for: Christiansburg, Roanoke, New River Valley, Blacksburg

**Best Choice Roofing** (national chain)
- Title format: "[City] Roofers | Best Choice Roofing"
- Keywords targeted: "roofers," city names, Owens Corning branding
- Weakness: National chain — can't match local trust signals
- Has dedicated pages for: Christiansburg, Blacksburg (nested under /roanoke/)

**NRV Roofing** (local since 1995)
- Keywords targeted: "New River Valley roofing," Montgomery/Pulaski/Floyd counties
- Weakness: Dated website, limited online presence, no strong SEO strategy

**Henderson Roofing** (local since 1994)
- Keywords targeted: "Christiansburg," "Blacksburg," "New River Valley"
- Weakness: Minimal digital marketing presence

**220 Roofing & Restoration** (Roanoke)
- Keywords targeted: "roofing company roanoke," "roofing contractor roanoke va"
- Strength: Veteran-owned angle, 25-year workmanship guarantee

### Keyword Gap Opportunities for MDR

1. **GAF Master Elite keywords** — Almost no competitors target these. Own "GAF Master Elite contractor [city]," "best roof warranty virginia"
2. **Timber Seal keywords** — Zero competition. Own this niche entirely
3. **Slate/specialty roofing** — Very few competitors offer slate or terra-cotta
4. **24/7 emergency** — Not all competitors advertise round-the-clock availability
5. **Smith Mountain Lake** — Underserved market with affluent homeowners
6. **Covington / Lexington / Wytheville** — Minimal roofing SEO competition in these smaller markets
7. **"Cost in Virginia" content** — Competitors don't have Virginia-specific pricing guides
