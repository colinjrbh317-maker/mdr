# SOP: Post-Close Drip Campaigns

## When This Applies
Job is fully closed (paid in full). These are past customers who may need additional services or can refer others.

## Pipeline Stage
**Closed** (completed and paid)

## Drip Sequence

| Touch | Timing | Channel | Content |
|-------|--------|---------|---------|
| D1 | +7 days after close | Email | Thank you + review request link |
| D2 | +30 days | Text | "How's the new roof? Any questions?" |
| D3 | +90 days | Email | Seasonal checkup + gutter/siding/window cross-sell |
| D4 | +180 days | Email | Referral program ask ($250 per referral) |
| D5 | +365 days | Email | Anniversary check-in + maintenance/inspection offer |

## Past-Customer Outreach Rules (CRITICAL)
Before contacting any past customer, verify:
- Customer paid in full
- No unresolved disputes
- No history of hostile interactions
- MDR team had a positive working experience
- Homeowner was compliant and respectful during project

If ANY red flags: do NOT contact. Escalate to management.

## "Do NOT Offer" Items
Never offer:
- Services already provided recently
- Items under warranty
- Repairs MDR already handled for free
- Past requests the homeowner declined
- Anything outside MDR policy

## Referral Program Details
- $250 reward for any referral that leads to a full replacement
- No limit on referrals
- Reward delivered as gift card or check upon job completion and payment
- Marketing takes photo with referrer for social media post

## AI Agent Role at This Stage
- Track closed date and advance through drip cadence automatically
- Draft personalized messages based on what services were performed (don't offer roofing to someone who just got a new roof, offer gutters, siding, windows instead)
- Tailor seasonal offers based on time of year
- Log all drip messages back to AccuLynx job record

## Communication Tone
Casual, grateful, non-pushy. These are happy customers, nurture the relationship.

## Example Messages

**D2 (30 days, Text):**
"Hi [Name], this is Modern Day Roofing. Just checking in, how's the new roof holding up? Let us know if you have any questions. We're always here!"

**D4 (180 days, Email):**
"Hi [Name], hope you're doing well! We wanted to let you know about our referral program, if you know anyone who needs roofing work, we'll send you $250 when their project is complete. No limits! Just give them our number or reply to this email with their info."
