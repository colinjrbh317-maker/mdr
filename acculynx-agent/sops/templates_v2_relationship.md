# MDR AI Sales Agent, Follow-Up Message Templates v2 (Relationship-First)

> **Version:** 2.0, Relationship-Focused
> **Author:** MDR AI Sales Agent SOP Library
> **Date:** March 31, 2026
> **Philosophy:** Every message should feel like a trusted neighbor checking in, not a salesperson chasing a deal. We're protecting homes here in the New River Valley, and that's worth communicating clearly.

---

## How to Use These Templates

- **TEXT:** Under 300 characters, casual-professional, no emojis, no all caps. Always identify MDR and rep name on first contact.
- **EMAIL:** Includes Subject line, professional body, and branded signature block. Proper grammar throughout.
- **Variables:** Replace `[Name]`, `[Rep Name]`, `[Position]`, `[Date]`, `[Time]` with actual values before sending.
- **Signature block** (use on all emails):

```
Best regards,
[Rep Name]
[Position]
Modern Day Roofing
540-553-6007
[rep]@moderndayroof.com
www.moderndayroof.com
```
