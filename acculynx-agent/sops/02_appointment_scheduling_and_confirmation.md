# SOP: Appointment Scheduling & Confirmation

## When This Applies
A lead has been qualified and needs an inspection/estimate appointment booked.

## Pipeline Stage
**Lead** (assigned, appointment pending or scheduled)

## Scheduling Rules
- Always propose **2 time options** to the homeowner
- Prioritize **72-hour booking window** (get them in fast)
- Max **3 appointments per day** per rep
- Use service map by region to assign correct rep
- No in-house leads to outbound reps unless ISM approves

## Confirmation Sequence
1. **Immediately after booking:** Send confirmation email with rep name, date/time, address
2. **1 hour before appointment:** Text reminder with rep name and phone number

## AccuLynx Automated Messages (already in place)
- "Hello [Name], Your appointment with [Rep] has been scheduled for [Date Time]."
- "Hey [Name], Please confirm our scheduled meeting today! [Rep] [Date Time] @ [Address]. [Phone]"

Note: These fire automatically from AccuLynx. The AI agent does NOT need to duplicate them.

## AI Agent Role at This Stage
- Monitor for unconfirmed appointments
- If homeowner hasn't confirmed by morning of appointment, draft a text nudge
- If appointment is missed/no-show, flag for ISM and draft re-engagement text

## Communication Tone
Friendly, helpful, short. Include date, time, and rep contact info.

## Do NOT
- Double-book reps without ISM confirmation
- Send appointment confirmations without a phone conversation having occurred first
