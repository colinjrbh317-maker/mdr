# SOP: Post-Inspection Follow-Up

## When This Applies
Rep has visited the property, conducted inspection, and either left proposals or needs to send them.

## Pipeline Stage
**Lead → Prospect** transition

## Timing Rules
- Rep enters notes + uploads photos in AccuLynx **before leaving the driveway**
- Estimates delivered within **24 hours** of appointment
- ISM sends official estimate email with attachments (estimate, style guide, warranty info)
- If proposals not left at appointment: email/text same day saying "please keep an eye on your email for the proposal"

## Follow-Up Messages

### Same-Day (after inspection):
**Email from rep:**
"Hello [Name], Thank you for taking time to meet with me today. Per our conversation, please find attached the [partial/total] roof replacement estimates. Please give me a call if you have any questions. Thank you, [Rep] [Phone]"

### If No Reply After 48 Hours:
ISM begins nurture sequence (see SOP 04).

## What Rep Must Document Before Leaving
- Do you need to create additional estimates?
- Did you promise any extra work or upgrades?
- Was the homeowner expecting other estimates from competitors?
- Is the homeowner ready now, or follow up later?

## AI Agent Role at This Stage
- Monitor for jobs that moved to Prospect but haven't had an estimate email sent within 24 hours → alert ISM
- Draft the post-inspection thank-you email if rep hasn't sent one by end of day
- Begin tracking for the nurture cadence (SOP 04) starting at 48 hours

## Communication Tone
Professional, grateful, confident. Reference what was discussed. Include phone number for questions.

## Do NOT
- Send estimates unless they are 100% ready (no drafts to homeowners)
- Discuss pricing changes via text without COO/ISM approval
