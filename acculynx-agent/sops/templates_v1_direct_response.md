# MDR AI Sales Agent, Follow-Up Message Templates v1
## Direct Response Edition

**Compiled:** March 31, 2026
**Purpose:** Outbound message templates for every pipeline layer and touch point
**Voice:** Direct, helpful, real-person tone. No emojis. No all caps.
**Character limit:** Text messages under 300 characters. Email includes subject line + signature.
**Placeholders:** [Name] = homeowner first name, [Rep] = rep full name, [RepFirst] = rep first name, [Phone] = rep direct line, [Date] = specific date, [Time] = specific time, [Service] = specific service discussed (e.g. "roof replacement," "repair estimate," "gutter inspection")

## Hard Stop Rules (No Message Sent)

- Customer has replied STOP via SMS, permanent SMS block, no further texts ever
- Customer explicitly requested no contact, permanent stop, all channels
- Lead marked "No damage / Not a fit", no follow-up
- Layer 11 Touch 3 sent with no response, file permanently closed, never contact again
- Customer expresses anger, makes a threat, or raises a legal concern, escalate to COO immediately, AI sends nothing
