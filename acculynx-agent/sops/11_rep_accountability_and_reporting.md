# SOP: Rep Accountability & Reporting Metrics

## When This Applies
Used by the reporting dashboard to track team performance and AI agent effectiveness.

## Weekly Rep Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| New Leads Entered | 30-40 |
| Appointments Booked | 25+ |
| Estimates Sent | 20-30 |
| No-Shows Tracked | All documented |
| Review Links Sent | 10+ |
| CRM Status Updates | 100% current |
| Contracts Signed | 3+/month per rep |
| Reviews Received | 1+/week per rep |

## Rep Submission (Weekly, Every Friday)
Each rep submits:
- Leads run
- Estimates sent
- Contracts signed
- Reviews received
- Referrals collected
- Follow-up attempts
- Photos uploaded (Y/N)
- Used Roof-in-a-Bag on all? (Y/N)
- DFT used? (Y/N)

## AI Agent Dashboard Metrics
The reporting dashboard should show:

### Pipeline Health
- Total active leads by milestone
- Stale leads count (48h+ without activity)
- Average time in each milestone stage
- Conversion rates: Lead → Prospect → Approved → Closed

### Rep Performance
- Leads assigned vs. leads contacted within 1 day
- Response time to new leads
- Follow-up compliance (are they hitting the cadence?)
- Contracts signed per rep per month

### AI Agent Activity
- Messages drafted today/this week
- Messages approved by reps vs. auto-sent
- Messages edited by reps (shows where AI voice needs tuning)
- Leads re-engaged by AI that converted
- Escalations triggered

### Commission Tracking
- Jobs closed per rep
- Revenue per rep
- Commission calculated based on MDR's commission structure

## Weekly Audit
- ISM & COO randomly check 3-5 CRM notes and CallRail interactions weekly
- Team members with no documented communication flagged for review
- "Silent files" (no activity or notes) flagged automatically by the AI agent

## AI Agent Role
- Automatically generate weekly reports from AccuLynx data + internal tracking
- Flag reps who are below targets
- Highlight AI-recovered leads that converted (ROI proof)
