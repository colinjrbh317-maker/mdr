# SOP: Lead Intake & Qualification

## When This Applies
A new lead has just entered the system, phone call, web form, referral, or walk-in.

## Pipeline Stage
**Lead** (unassigned or just assigned)

## Timing Rules
- Web form leads: call back within **3 minutes**
- Phone leads: answer within **1-2 rings**
- CRM entry by DFI: within **10 minutes** of contact
- ISM lead assignment: within **30 minutes**
- Sales rep first contact: within **same day** (preferably 1 hour)

## First Contact Protocol
On first contact attempt, ALL three channels must be used:
1. **Phone call** (primary), leave voicemail if no answer
2. **Text message**, friendly intro with name + company
3. **Email**, confirmation with rep info

## Qualification Questions (DFI asks these)
- Full name, phone, email, address
- Type of service needed (replacement, repair, inspection, gutters)
- Age of roof
- Timeline / urgency
- Any other estimates received?
- Are they the decision-maker?

## CRM Requirements
- Lead source must be tagged (Google, Facebook, Referral, Yard Sign, etc.)
- All call notes documented in AccuLynx
- Status set to "Lead, Assigned to [Rep Name]"
- Tags applied: [Urgent], [Out of Area], [HOA], [Storm], [Insurance], etc.

## AI Agent Role at This Stage
- Monitor new leads for response time compliance
- Flag leads that haven't been contacted within 1 hour of assignment
- Draft initial outreach text if rep hasn't made contact by end of day

## Communication Tone
Professional, warm, brief. Always include rep name and company name on first contact.

**Example text:**
"Hi [Name], this is [Rep] with Modern Day Roofing. Just confirming your appointment for [time] tomorrow. Looking forward to meeting you!"

## Do NOT
- Book appointments via text without a phone call first
- Leave notes blank in CRM
- Self-assign leads without ISM permission
