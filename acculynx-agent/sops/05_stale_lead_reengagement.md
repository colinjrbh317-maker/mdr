# SOP: Stale Lead Re-Engagement

## When This Applies
Leads that have gone cold and weren't converted through the normal nurture sequence. These are leads being revisited after an extended period of inactivity.

## Pipeline Stage
**Lead or Prospect** with no activity in 14+ days (beyond the normal nurture cadence)

## Stale Lead Categories
- **No Response:** Never picked up or scheduled
- **Estimate Sent, No Signature:** Estimate delivered but no follow-up response
- **Ghosted:** Initial interest shown, then no further communication
- **Undecided:** Expressed need for time but didn't re-engage
- **Cold Leads:** No contact in over 14 days, not marked as closed

## Re-Engagement Approach
Before reaching out, review:
- Lead's complete history and previous interactions
- Services they were interested in and any objections
- Whether MDR can offer solutions, promotions, or new scheduling options

## AI Agent Role at This Stage
- Identify leads matching stale categories from AccuLynx data
- Draft personalized re-engagement messages using full lead context
- Tone: casual check-in, NOT aggressive sales pitch
- Reference their specific situation if possible

## Re-Engagement Script Template

**Opening:**
"Hi [Name], this is [Rep Name] from Modern Day Roofing. I wanted to check in regarding your roofing project. Are you still considering getting that taken care of?"

**If they respond, build rapport:**
"I understand that timing and choosing the right contractor are crucial decisions. Can you share what's most important to you in this project?"

**Address concerns:**
"Based on what you've shared, it sounds like [restate their concern] is a top priority. We specialize in [specific solution] to meet that exact need."

**Soft close:**
"We currently have openings this week. Would you like to take advantage of the availability?"

## Communication Tone
Warm, low-pressure, helpful. Frame as a check-in, not a sales pitch. Acknowledge that time has passed.

## Do NOT
- Contact leads marked "Do Not Contact"
- Re-offer services that were already completed
- Spam, if they don't respond after 2-3 re-engagement attempts, stop
