# SOP: Post-Install, Walkthrough, Review, Close

## When This Applies
Roof installation is complete. Job moves through Completed → Invoiced → Closed.

## Pipeline Stage
**Completed → Invoiced → Closed**

## Final Walkthrough Scheduling
- DFI calls homeowner within **24 hours** of job marked "Complete"
- Target: walkthrough scheduled within **72 hours** of job finish
- Try **3 times over 2 days** before escalating to ISM
- Walkthrough script: "Hi [Name], this is [Name] with Modern Day Roofing. I'm calling to schedule your final walkthrough. One of our project consultants will stop by for a quick 5-10 minute visit to confirm everything looks great. Would [2 date options] work for you?"

## Post-Install Rep Follow-Up
Rep texts customer 7-10 days after install:
"Hey [Name], this is [Rep] with Modern Day Roofing. I just wanted to reach out and see how everything went with the installation. Do you have any concerns?"

## Review Request
- Send review request **same day** after install or at walkthrough
- Include direct Google Review link
- "We'd love to hear about your experience! If you were satisfied with our work, please leave us a review here: [Link]"

## Invoice & Payment
- ISM generates invoice based on final worksheet
- Invoice sent day of job completion (preferred)
- If unpaid: "Hello [Name], your roofing project is complete, and we've emailed your invoice. Please confirm receipt."

## AI Agent Role at This Stage
- Monitor completed jobs that haven't had walkthrough scheduled within 24 hours → alert DFI
- Draft post-install satisfaction check text if rep hasn't sent one within 7 days
- Draft review request message
- Monitor unpaid invoices and draft gentle payment reminders

## Communication Tone
Grateful, warm, brief. This is the wrap-up, make them feel valued.

## Do NOT
- Ask for reviews before the job is fully complete
- Be aggressive about payment, professional and patient
- Forget to log the walkthrough outcome in CRM
