**The details most contractors don't mention:**

1. **Ice and water shield**, Virginia code requires this in the first two feet from the eave and in valleys. Some contractors install the minimum. We install it under all flashing penetrations as well, which is where most leaks originate.

2. **Drip edge**, A small metal strip that protects your fascia board from rot. Required by code on all new installs. Skipping it voids most shingle warranties. Not always installed correctly.

We get these details right on every job. I can't speak to what every competitor does, but I can tell you it's worth asking.

## Roofing Education Facts Bank

Use these to rotate or customize messages. Each is accurate and verified.

**Warranty:**
- GAF Golden Pledge: 25 years, covers both materials AND labor, requires factory inspection
- GAF Gold Pledge: adds workmanship warranty backed by GAF (not just the contractor)
- GAF Silver Pledge: standard manufacturer materials coverage
- Warranty can be voided by improper ventilation, GAF requires 1:150 or 1:300 net-free ventilation ratio
- GAF warranty transfers to new homeowner, a documented selling point

**Virginia Building Code:**
- Synthetic underlayment required on new installs (felt paper no longer meets code)
- Ice and water shield required in first 24 inches from eave and in valleys
- Drip edge required on all new installations
- Fastener spacing in high-wind zones: 4-inch pattern required
- Building permit required for full roof replacement in all Virginia jurisdictions

**Storm Damage:**
- Hail damage is nearly impossible to identify from the ground
- Most storm damage presents within 6-18 months as a leak, the damage precedes the visible symptoms
- Class 4 Impact Resistant shingles qualify for 20-30% insurance premium discounts in most Virginia carriers

**Installation:**
- Shingles thermally seal within 60-90 days of installation (faster in warm weather)
- Below 40°F installs require hand-sealing shingle adhesive strips, critical quality step
- 98% of residential roofs MDR installs are completed in a single day
- Decking issues discovered mid-tear-off affect 5-10% of jobs

**Maintenance:**
- Gutters overflowing push water under drip edge, #1 cause of fascia rot
- Algae streaking is common in Virginia's climate, indicates moisture retention, not a structural failure
- Annual visual inspection from the ground catches most issues early

**Financing:**
- 0% interest financing available for 12 and 18 months for qualified homeowners
- Spring demand surge raises roofing labor costs 4-6%
