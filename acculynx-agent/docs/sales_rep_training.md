- **Approve** (green): sends the message as-is, logs an AccuLynx note.

## What the AI knows

The AI reads everything in AccuLynx automatically:
- Job notes you type
- Milestone change comments
- Emails and texts visible in the Communications tab
- Estimate dates and revisions
- Initial inspection dates

**The single best thing you can do** is paste the **Rilla AI summary** into the job notes OR into the portal's `Transcript` tab right after every meeting. The richer the context, the better the drafts.

### Shorthand the AI understands

If you write `Left VM 5/12, HO wants FR`, the AI translates that to the homeowner as `"After my voicemail Monday — you mentioned wanting a full replacement."` The acronyms it knows:

| Shorthand | Means |
|---|---|
| VM | voicemail |
| HO | homeowner |
| FR | full replacement |
| RR | roof repair |
| EST | estimate / proposal |
| PP | Platinum Pledge (GAF warranty) |
| GAF | GAF (brand name — kept) |

The AI never exposes these acronyms to the homeowner. Use whatever you'd normally use.

## When the homeowner replies

Three paths:

1. **Objective question** ("what's your warranty?", "how does Hearth financing work?", "what time are you here Friday?")
   → AI replies on its own within 2-3 minutes. You're BCC'd.
   → AccuLynx note: `AUTO-REPLY (objective <topic> answered, rep BCC'd)`.

2. **They want to back out** ("we're selling the house", "we went with someone else", "take us off the list")
   → AI sends a one-line empathy template, pauses the cadence permanently, logs to AccuLynx.

3. **Pricing, objections, complaints, or anything the AI isn't sure about**
   → Email to you, Sierra CC'd.
   → Cadence is paused. You handle it from your inbox. When you resolve it, manually resume the lead in AccuLynx or update the notes and the AI takes over again.

## Quick questions

**Q: Can the AI cancel a deal?**
No. If the homeowner says anything tense, it routes to you.

**Q: What if I'm at a job site at 6 PM?**
The AI auto-sends the original draft. If you don't want it to go, **Skip** in the portal before 6 PM. (Skip is one tap.)

**Q: What if I want to send a custom message right now, not from the queue?**
That feature isn't built yet (separate project). For now, you can paste a transcript via the `Transcript` tab and that influences the next draft, or you can just text the homeowner from your phone like normal.

**Q: How do I know it's an AI draft and not Sierra typed it?**
Every send shows up as `[AI Agent]` in the AccuLynx Communications tab. Look for the prefix.

**Q: What if the AI says something dumb?**
Edit it. Every edit you make trains future drafts. After a few weeks the voice will match you. If it's a pattern, message Colin so we can update the prompt.
